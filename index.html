Idaho

Idaho is renowned for its stunning mountainous landscapes and vast expanses of protected wilderness, making it a haven for outdoor recreation. The state is famous for its potatoes and is home to the rare star garnet, found almost exclusively here. Despite being predominantly landlocked, Idaho boasts a small seaport in Lewiston.
